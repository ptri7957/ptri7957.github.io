ABOUT US

Discover some of the South Coast’s finest self-contained holiday villas at Ocean Grove in Bawley Point. Set amongst 26 acres of beautiful spotted gum forest and natural bush land are five architecturally designed one or two bedroom villas.

All of our villas feature contemporary furnishings and have been thoughtfully appointed with your ultimate comfort and convenience foremost in mind. With spacious lounge/dining areas, full sized kitchens, comfortable bedrooms and modern bathrooms, two of which contain generous corner spas, each villa offers everything that you need to make the most of your time away.

Situated right in the heart of Bawley Point we are merely minutes from the local shops and only a short drive to a number of Bawley’s most stunning beaches. Whether you’re looking for a weekend getaway or an extended holiday haven, Ocean Grove should be your first choice for luxury accommodation in Bawley Point. Discover more.
