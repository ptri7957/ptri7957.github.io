HOW TO GET HERE

Ocean Grove Villas is situated approximately 2.5 hours from Canberra and 3.5 hours from Sydney.

To get to Bawley Point turn off the Princes Highway onto Bawley Point Road at Termeil, which is situated between Batemans Bay and Ulladulla. Continue along Bawley Point Road for approximately 5 kilometres and then turn right onto Murramarang Road. Continue along Murramarang Road for approximately 1.5 kilometres then turn right onto Forster Drive.

Ocean Grove Villas is on the left hand side at the top of Forster Drive.
