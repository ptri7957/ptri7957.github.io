TERMS AND CONDITIONS

  1. ACCEPTANCE & RESPONSIBILITY

    2. Payment of the Deposit constitutes acceptance of these Terms and Conditions.

  2. RATES AND ADDITIONAL CHARGES

    3. All rates are per Villa and per night. For all single bedroom villas, the rate is for two (2) people and for all two bedroom villas the rate is for up to four (4) people. Additional people may be included upon request and by prior arrangement with Management. An additional person (whether adult or child) will attract an additional charge (see Rates for further information on charges). A limited number of portable cots are available for hire.

  3. PAYMENT POLICY

    4. At the time of booking, a deposit equal to the first night’s accommodation for each villa booked must be paid and will be charged to the provided credit card. If the credit card provided is deemed invalid, the booking will not be confirmed until a valid credit card is provided. Any remaining balance of the booking will be charged on the day of arrival.

  4. CANCELLATION POLICY

    5. If you wish to vary or cancel your Booking, please contact us immediately on +61 2 4457 2059 or via email to admin@interludes.com.au. Your deposit is non-refundable in the event of a cancellation.

    If you have paid more than the deposit or paid in full and cancel your Booking your refund will be calculated on the following basis:

    • For all bookings made during the Low or Mid seasons, a full refund (less deposit) will be made

    • For all bookings made during the High season, and provided that notice of a cancellation occurs more than 48 hours prior to the booked date of arrival, charges equivalent to two (2) night’s accommodation will be deducted from your payment and the remainder will be refunded.

    • For all bookings made during the High season, and where notice of a cancellation is given less than 48 hours prior to the booked date of arrival, charges equivalent to three (3) night’s accommodation will be deducted from your payment and the remainder will be refunded.

    If Management is able to relet the Villa for the period cancelled a further refund may be made less administration charges. Should you be eligible for a refund it will be made through your chosen payment method at time of Booking. An administration charge of $50.00 may be charged for any variation or cancellation of a Booking.

  5. CHECK IN/OUT

    6. Check-in time is not before 2:00pm on the date of arrival and check out time is not later than 10:00am on the date of departure, unless by prior arrangement with Management. Early check-in or late departure is subject to availability and extra charges may apply.

    You must notify Management of your expected arrival time and a mobile contact number at least two (2) days before arrival.

    Check-in/check-out and key collection/return procedure will be as follows unless special arrangements have been made prior to arrival. On arrival at the Property, attend the Office to collect keys and complete registration. On the day of departure, all keys must be returned to the Office by the agreed check-out time.

  6. PETS

    7. Some small to medium sized pets may be allowed at the Property by prior arrangement with Management. All pets attract a charge of $15.00 per pet per night. A maximum of two pets are allowed per booking.

    Pet owners are responsible for cleaning up after their pets both within and outside the Property. While Pets may be allowed within a Villa they are not allowed on any furniture at any time. Any evidence of pets on furniture may incur extra cleaning fees (see Rates for details of charges).

    Pets must not be left alone or unattended in a Villa and must be secured at all times when out on the premises. Pet owners are responsible for the safety of their pets and for ensuring that they do not create an undue disturbance to other guests staying at the Property.

  7. LINEN AND TOWELS

    8. We provide bed linen, pillows, blankets and towels (bath and beach) which must be left where supplied in the bedrooms or bathroom on departure. Additional cleaning and linen change may be organised upon request and by prior arrangement with Management but may be subject to an additional cost.

  8. YOUR OTHER RESPONSIBILITIES

    9. Smoking is not permitted inside the Villas or generally on the Property premises but is permitted out on the deck of your Villa. Only the guests nominated and agreed in the Booking may stay in the Property over-night. If any other guests stay, extra charges may apply or the agreement may be terminated without refund.

    Disturbance to other guests and/or our neighbours, including excessive noise, is prohibited and may result in termination and eviction without refund and extra charges may be made for security and other expenses.

    Before departure, all rubbish must be put in the appropriate rubbish bins provided and crockery and cutlery washed and packed away. The Villa must be left in a clean and tidy condition.

    Extra cleaning charges may be incurred for the cleaning of dirty dishes, washing machine, dishwasher and removal of excessive rubbish etc. Should the cleaning fee be more than the usual cost for cleaning the property, you will be charged the additional costs over and above the normal cleaning fee which will be charged to your credit card. All furniture and furnishings must be left in the position they were in when you arrived.

    We acknowledge that accidental damage to/of items can occur however, all damages incurred will be assessed by Management. If damages are deemed to be extreme or excessive, we reserve the right to charge you for replacement or repair costs, against the credit card you have provided.

    The property should be vacated on time and secured. All windows and doors are to be locked. All keys must be returned to Management at the Office or as otherwise directed.
