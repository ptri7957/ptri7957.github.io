RATES

All rates described below are per villa and per night. For on-line bookings, a minimum stay of two (2) nights applies to all villas (except during the high season where a minimum of three (3) nights apply). Please contact us regarding availability if you wish to enquire about bookings for less than two (2) nights. For all single bedroom villas the rate is for two (2) people and for all two (2) bedroom villas the rate is for up to four (4) people. An additional person (whether adult or child) will attract an additional charge (see below). For maximum occupancies for each villa, please see information on the villas page.

We are the perfect accommodation for all travellers, including ones who like to take their furry companions with them when they venture from home. If this describes you, please enquire with us about accommodating your pet. For a modest additional charge we accept some small to medium size pets on the property and within the cottages when they are under the care and the supervision of their owners (see table below for charges).
